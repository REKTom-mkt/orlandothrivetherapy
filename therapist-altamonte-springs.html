Therapy Services in Altamonte Springs

Couples Therapy & Marriage Counseling

Relationship challenges don't discriminate by zip code. Our Gottman-trained therapists help Altamonte Springs couples improve communication, resolve recurring conflicts, and rebuild emotional connection. Whether you're newlyweds adjusting to married life or long-term partners who've grown apart, we can help.

Individual Therapy

Life in the suburbs comes with its own pressures: long commutes, demanding jobs, raising children, and sometimes feeling isolated despite being surrounded by people. Individual therapy provides a confidential space to process emotions, develop coping strategies, and work toward the life you want.

Anxiety Treatment

Worry that won't stop. Difficulty sleeping. A constant feeling that something bad is about to happen. Anxiety affects people across all walks of life, and our Altamonte Springs therapists specialize in evidence-based treatments that actually work.

EMDR Therapy

Trauma doesn't have to control your life forever. EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) helps your brain process difficult memories so they no longer trigger overwhelming emotions. Our certified EMDR therapists have helped clients heal from accidents, abuse, loss, and other traumatic experiences.

Women's Therapy

Women often carry invisible burdens: caretaking for children and aging parents, managing households while building careers, and losing themselves in the process. Our female therapists create a space where women can explore these challenges without judgment.

Life Coaching

Sometimes you don't need therapy—you need direction. Our life coaches help with career transitions, goal setting, work-life balance, and creating a roadmap for the future you want.
