Therapy Services in Winter Park

Our Winter Park therapists specialize in a range of mental health concerns. Whatever you're facing, we have a counselor with the expertise to help.

Couples Therapy & Marriage Counseling

Relationships take work, and sometimes couples need professional support to navigate challenges. Our Gottman-trained therapists help partners improve communication, rebuild trust, and reconnect emotionally. Whether you're dealing with frequent arguments, emotional distance, or recovering from infidelity, we're here to help.

Individual Therapy

Life doesn't always go as planned. Individual therapy provides a confidential space to process difficult emotions, develop coping strategies, and work toward personal growth. Our therapists help with depression, life transitions, self-esteem, grief, and finding direction when you feel stuck.

Anxiety Treatment

Anxiety affects millions of adults, and Winter Park's high-achieving community is no exception. Racing thoughts, constant worry, difficulty sleeping, and physical symptoms like chest tightness can significantly impact your quality of life. Our therapists use proven techniques including cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) to help you manage anxiety effectively.

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is a powerful therapy for trauma, PTSD, and disturbing memories that continue to affect your present life. EMDR helps your brain process traumatic experiences so they no longer trigger intense emotional reactions.

Women's Therapy

Women face unique challenges throughout different life stages. From career pressures and relationship stress to motherhood, hormonal changes, and identity shifts, our female therapists understand the complexities women navigate.

Life Coaching

Sometimes you don't need therapy for a mental health diagnosis—you need guidance, accountability, and support to reach your goals. Our life coaches help with career decisions, personal development, work-life balance, and creating the life you want.
