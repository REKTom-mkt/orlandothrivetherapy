Charlotte Meier, MA, RMHCI

Charlotte Meier is a Registered Mental Health Counselor Intern (RMHCI) in the state of Florida. She works exclusively with clients in the Orlando area in private practice at Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching and Counseling at the Winter Park counseling office.

Who Charlotte Works With & Charlotte's Approach

Charlotte shares the Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching and Counseling philosophy by using an integrative approach to therapy and a mix of therapeutic interventions to support her clients in their individualized therapeutic journey. She uses evidence-based techniques including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Positive Psychology, and Client-Centered Therapy to help her clients reach their therapy goals quickly. Her specialties include working with women and men who are experiencing anxiety, panic, OCD (obsessive compulsive disorder), depression, grief, life transitions, relationship issues, and stress from work or family pressures. She implements other techniques such as mindfulness-based practices and emotion-focused therapies. Her approach to therapy focuses on helping her clients with the alignment of core values, thoughts and behaviors to help them overcome faulty thinking patterns and live more purposeful lives.

Charlotte believes in the importance of understanding each client as a whole person, not as the problems that they experience. She enjoys working with individuals from diverse backgrounds including members of the LGBTQIA+ community, individuals of every gender, various cultural identities, and age ranges. She is dedicated to helping clients navigate anxiety, depression, and trauma. She also works with clients to improve their self-esteem, learn coping skills, identify their strengths, challenge negative thought patterns, regulate emotions, and work through difficult life changes. Charlotte believes in tailoring her approach for each client to fit their specific needs and preferences. This includes creating an emotionally safe space for clients to emotionally explore, grow, and thrive.

Please contact Charlotte to find out more about her approach and to schedule an intake appointment and get started today.

Note about insurance: Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching, and Counseling is a self-pay practice because we do not believe that your quality of care should be dictated by your insurance company. Charlotte offers individualized treatment plans which incorporate a unique combination of expert psychotherapy with mindfulness and lifestyle coaching to achieve lasting results. Her rates are based on the services you receive and on your unique circumstance.

What to Expect

When you schedule your first session, you will be sent a link to complete electronic paperwork. The first session will focus on getting to know one another and identifying your goals for therapy. This will help to determine your overall plan and how often you will need to meet for therapy. Charlotte welcomes her clients, without judgement, into the safety of a private, confidential setting where deep healing can be possible.

Rates

Charlottes's rates for a 50-90 minute therapy session are based on the service you receive and will be discussed during your initial contact. Charlotte offers sliding scale options on a limited basis as well as discounted membership options and will discuss this with you during your initial appointment. Payment is due at the time of session.

Charlotte's Clinical Background

Charlotte is a UCF graduate with a Master of Arts in Clinical Psychology and is a Registered Mental Health Counselor Intern in the State of Florida. She has worked clinically in diverse environments including inpatient and intensive outpatient settings with clients with a wide range of therapeutic needs. Beyond her professional qualifications, Charlotte is an advocate for mental health awareness and is passionate about lifelong learning. She attends conferences and workshops as well as reads books and articles to continue to keep her knowledge of therapeutic techniques current and relevant to best support her clients so they can thrive.
