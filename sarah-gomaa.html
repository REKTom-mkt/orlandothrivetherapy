Sarah Gomaa, MA, LMHC

Sarah Gomaa is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor in the state of Florida. She works exclusively with individuals through therapy in the Orlando area in private practice at Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching, and Counseling at our Winter Park location.

Who Sarah Works With + Sarah's Approach

Sarah utilizes an eclectic approach to therapy while drawing on a variety of interventions such as CBT, EFT, Psychodynamic, and other therapies targeted to help individuals and couples. When working with individuals, Sarah focuses on core issues of co-dependence, boundaries, core beliefs, self-esteem, relationship issues, childhood trauma or family system dysfunction, among others. She creates an integrative action plan to dive into the root issues and create deep insight for change. Sarah enjoys working with women and men from diverse backgrounds and communities and also incorporates the use of culturally informed therapeutic techniques.

In Sarah's work with her couples, she utilizes her foundation as a certified Level 3 Gottman Method expert to establish a solid framework to begin on. She works with all couples; Married, cohabitating, dating, or engaged, blended families, and all others who are experiencing conflict and do not know where to turn. This could include issues with trust, infidelity, miscommunication, contemplating divorce, dissatisfied partner in the relationship, in-law/family issues, or co-parenting issues. Sarah also uses additional therapeutic techniques such as Imago Therapy, Emotionally Focused Therapy, and when appropriate, Discernment Counseling. This allows her to use an all-encompassing approach based on her clients needs to focus on deepening connections, while processing emotional wounds, resolving conflict, and improving communication.

Sarah views therapy as a partnership, believing that everyone can overcome any obstacles they face with the right tools and guidance. With her passion for lasting growth and change, she aids in guiding her clients to understand the root cause of behaviors, validation, and empower awareness of their needs, values, and emotions, leading towards the ability to thrive!

Please contact Sarah to find out more about her approach and to schedule an intake appointment and get started today.

Note about insurance: Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching, and Counseling therapists do not work for insurance companies. We work exclusively for you and are a self-pay practice. We use a combination of psychotherapy, life coaching, mindfulness, and therapeutic techniques which provides you with a holistic approach for the best outcome. Sarah's rates are based on the services she offers, and she will discuss with you the best course of treatment for your unique circumstance during your initial appointment.

What to Expect

When you schedule your first session, you will be sent a link to complete electronic paperwork. The first session will focus on getting to know one another and identifying your goals for therapy. This will help to determine your overall plan and how often you will need to meet for therapy. Sarah's counseling space will welcome you immediately and her open demeanor will encourage you to feel instantly safe.

Rates

Sarah's rates for therapy vary based on the service provided. She offers prepaid memberships at a reduced rate and will gladly discuss this offer during your initial consultation. Payment is due at the time of your session.

Sarah's Clinical Background

Sarah is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor and holds a Master of Arts degree from the University of Central Florida in Clinical Psychology. Her work in private practice allows her to provide an expert, boutique experience to her clients who are seeking deep change and insight within a professional, confidential, safe setting. She has extensive training in many diverse areas that include addictions and recovery, narcissistic abuse, codependency issues, family system dysfunction, and emotional regulation issues. She enjoys working with any and all populations, as well as couples, and believes her open, honest approach creates a warm welcome so that her clients can quickly reach their highest potential for lasting change.
