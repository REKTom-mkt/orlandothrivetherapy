Questions For Your Insurance Provider Prior To Counseling

  • Will I have to be diagnosed with a mental health illness for you to cover my Orlando counseling sessions?
  • Will my employer know that I am in therapy or what my diagnosis is?
  • Does my insurance cover a family therapy session?
  • Does my insurance cover marital therapy or relationship counseling?
  • What if there aren’t any providers in your network that I want to see or they aren’t accepting new clients?
  • How can you authorize my therapy sessions with a mental health counselor that I choose on my own?
  • What is my out of pocket yearly deductible and has it been met?
  • Does my plan limit how many sessions I can attend for therapy?

IF YOU HAVE INSURANCE:
Prior to scheduling your appointment, please check with your insurance provider to first to determine your mental health coverage. Most insurance companies limit mental health treatment options, for this reason we do not take insurance. You may seek your own reimbursement from your insurance company.

Orlando Therapy Counseling Session Information

Session Duration

All sessions vary in duration, depending on the type of service (family counseling, career planning, or couples sessions might take more time), but generally your session will last 60 minutes in length.

Orlando Therapy Frequency

Orlando therapy frequency is established based on your individual needs and will be discussed at your first session. Many times clients find that weekly.

Office Hours

Our office hours for Orlando therapy are by appointment only and vary by provider. Extended evening or weekend appointments are available by request and availability.
