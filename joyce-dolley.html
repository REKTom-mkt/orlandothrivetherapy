Joyce Dolley, MS, RMHCI

Joyce Dolley is a Registered Mental Health Counselor Intern (RMHCI) with the state of Florida. Her greatest professional joy is helping individuals achieve their highest state of emotional well-being and personal growth. She works exclusively with Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching, and Counseling in Orlando at their Winter Park counseling office.

Who Joyce Works With & Joyce's Approach

Joyce specializes in working with individuals experiencing anxiety, depression, and trauma, as well as supporting couples and families in improving communication, resolving conflicts, and fostering stronger relationships. Her approach is rooted in evidence-based practices, specifically Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Person-Centered Therapy. These modalities help men and women seeking therapy to explore their experiences with compassion while building the skills and insights needed for meaningful change. Joyce is also trained in sand tray therapy and incorporates this expressive therapeutic technique when working with adults, as well as middle and high school age children.

Joyce brings additional expertise to help anyone seeking mental health therapy through her trainings in Trauma-Focused Behavioral Therapy, Social and Behavioral Training, Suicide Prevention, Psychological First Aid, Secondary Trauma Stress, and Skills for Psychological First Aid. These specialized skills enable her to provide effective support for individuals navigating crises, trauma recovery, and emotional challenges. She is also Gottman Level 1 trained for couples and incorporates EFT (Emotional Focused Therapy) to provide an integrative approach when working with couples facing infidelity, poor communication, issues with trust, intimacy, or contemplating divorce. She also works with blended families, step-parents, and family members seeking support for internal family systems.

Joyce's additional training in using expressive therapies, such as sand tray, further provides a non-invasive technique that clients can use to dive deeper into their repressed or paralyzing emotions from grief, trauma, rejection, or any change for any age.

Please contact Joyce to find out more about her approach and to schedule an intake appointment and get started today.

Note about insurance: Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching, and Counseling is a self-pay practice and we do not believe that your quality of care should be dictated by your insurance company. Joyce offers individualized treatment plans tailor fit for your specific needs. Her rates are based on the services you receive and on your unique circumstance.

What to Expect

When you schedule your first session, you will be sent a link to complete electronic paperwork. The first session will focus on getting to know one another and identifying your goals for therapy. This will help to determine your overall plan and how often you will need to meet for therapy. Joyce tailor fits her approach to meet her client's needs. This allows for a unique experience for each individual and provides the best environment for lasting change.

Rates

Joyce's rates for therapy vary based on the service provided. She does offer discounted membership plans that can help you save money and she will gladly discuss those options, along with her recommended course of action, during your initial intake session.

Joyce's Clinical Background

Joyce holds a Master’s degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Stetson University as well as a Master's degree in Human Resource Management from Barry University. Joyce is a Registered Mental Health Counseling Intern with the State of Florida. She has a diverse professional work history that has provided her with many opportunities for ongoing professional development to ensure the highest standard of care. Joyce will create a safe and supportive space where you will feel empowered to overcome challenges, build resilience, and THRIVE! She looks forward to working with you and being a part of your journey towards creating a fulfilling and balanced life.
