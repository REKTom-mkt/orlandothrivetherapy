Meet Heather Oller
LMHC, Owner

As a licensed psychotherapist for almost two decades, I have successfully taught couples and individuals how to feel happier, overcome barriers, and have better relationships with themselves and others. My passion to learn the best therapy techniques for anxiety, personal growth, and relationship issues came as a result of my own life experiences. These are personal areas in which I was able to overcome and navigate with success so empowering my clients in the same ways has been my greatest reward.

My approach to therapy is different then many counselors because I know what it takes for you to see results. First, I know that the relationship between the therapist and client is the most important element to achieving lasting change. Your goals are our goals! Secondly, I know that successful outcomes happen by using a combination of expert psychology with proven individualized therapy techniques and lifestyle coaching. This is one reason we do not accept insurance. If you want real lasting change to happen, you have to use the best therapy techniques for you...and you have to be willing to invest in it! We believe you are more than a diagnosis and worth the investment.

My vision for Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching, and Counseling happened as a result of wanting to empower more people. Since I am only one person, I could only help so many and do so much. Over the course of the last eight years, I began hand-selecting and training a team of like-minded counselors who now work beside me. Together we teach you how to navigate relationships better, develop better self-confidence, overcome anxiety, achieve personal growth, and discover how you can improve your overall quality of life. To put it simply, we teach you how to THRIVE, instead of just survive!
