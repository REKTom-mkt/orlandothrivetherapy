Riley Brickey, MS, RMHCI

Riley Brickey is a Registered Mental Health Counselor Intern (RMHCI) in the state of Florida and holds a Masters of Science degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling. She works exclusively with clients in the Orlando area in private practice at Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching and Counseling.

Who Riley Works With & Riley's Approach

Riley’s passion for therapy is personal. Shaped by both lived experience and professional training, she specializes in helping clients navigate anxiety, addiction, codependency, grief, perfectionism, self-esteem, trauma, and major life transitions. She understands the courage it takes to begin this work and considers it an honor to walk alongside clients as they move through it. In sessions, Riley serves as a collaborative partner, integrating evidence-based practices like CBT, ACT, expressive arts, and mindfulness with somatic techniques such as grounding, body awareness, and breathwork to support healing at both the cognitive and nervous system levels.

Whether you’re stuck in unhealthy patterns, feeling overwhelmed by anxiety, burdened by past trauma, or disconnected in your relationships, Riley meets you where you are, with empathy and curiosity. Together, you’ll explore the patterns and pain that are keeping you stuck and begin to create space for healing, clarity, and change. She believes that life’s challenges, no matter how big or small, deserve to be acknowledged. Therapy with Riley is more than just symptom management- it’s about building a life that feels true to you, where you feel more connected, empowered, and at home in yourself.

Please contact Riley to find out more about her approach and to schedule an intake appointment and get started today.

Note about insurance: Orlando Thrive Therapy, Coaching, and Counseling is a self-pay practice because we do not believe that your quality of care should be dictated by your insurance company. Riley offers individualized treatment plans which incorporate a unique combination of expert psychotherapy with mindfulness and lifestyle coaching to achieve lasting results. Her rates are based on the services you receive and on your unique circumstance.

What to Expect

When you schedule your first session, you will be sent a link to complete electronic paperwork. The first session will focus on getting to know one another and identifying your goals for therapy. This will help to determine your overall plan and how often you will need to meet for therapy. Riley provides a grounded, compassionate space for adults ready to do meaningful inner work. Her trauma-informed, person-centered, and relational approach is built on the belief that healing happens when you feel safe, seen, and supported.

Rates

Riley’s rates for a 50-60 minute therapy session are based on the service you receive and will be discussed during your initial contact. Riley offers discount membership options and will discuss this with you during your initial appointment. Payment is due at the time of session.

Riley's Clinical Background

Riley holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology from Clemson University, and a Master of Science degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Southern Methodist University, where she specialized in addiction and trauma. She has clinical experience across both inpatient and outpatient levels of care. Riley completed her graduate-level internship at an adult detention center, where she provided trauma-informed individual and crisis counseling to incarcerated clients, addressing a wide range of emotional, behavioral, and psychological challenges. Riley is passionate about creating a supportive and collaborative space where clients feel safe to explore their experiences, process trauma, and build lasting change. She integrates evidence-based practices with empathy and curiosity to help clients navigate life transitions, break free from unhelpful patterns, and foster resilience and growth.
